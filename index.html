About R&D Team

Exploring Innovation in Technology

The IEEE SRM R&D Team comprises passionate undergraduates dedicated to leveraging innovative technologies and collaborative expertise to pioneer groundbreaking solutions. Our research themes include Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics.

Led by the Head of the Domain (R&D), our team follows a structured hierarchy, emphasizing skill development and providing opportunities for growth, leadership roles, and professional advancement.

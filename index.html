About R&D Team

Exploring Innovation in Technology

The IEEE SRM R&D Team comprises passionate undergraduates dedicated to leveraging innovative technologies and collaborative expertise to pioneer groundbreaking solutions. Our research themes include Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics.

Led by the Head of the Domain (R&D), our team follows a structured hierarchy, emphasizing skill development and providing opportunities for growth, leadership roles, and professional advancement.

Join the IEEE Community for Innovation and Excellence

Joining IEEE opens a gateway to a world of opportunities, networking, and innovation. As the world's largest technical professional organization, IEEE offers an unparalleled platform for connecting with industry leaders, researchers, and peers globally. Engaging with IEEE means being part of a vibrant community dedicated to advancing technology for humanity's benefit. Membership grants access to cutting-edge research, conferences, workshops, and valuable resources, fostering professional growth and knowledge enrichment.

Benefits of Joining IEEE:

  • Access to top-tier research papers and publications
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts
  • Participation in global conferences, workshops, and events
  • Professional development through webinars and workshops
  • Exclusive access to career resources and job opportunities

Join us in shaping the future of technology and be part of a thriving community committed to innovation and excellence. Become an IEEE member today to unlock a world of possibilities and elevate your career in technology!

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore common questions related to IEEE SRM R&D Team recruitment and activities.

  • When does IEEE hold recruitment for R&D Domain?

    IEEE R&D Team holds recruitments once in every academic year, usually around September/October. Please do check our website for recruitment updates.

  • How do I apply for IEEE recruitment?

    Candidates can apply through an online application form, click on join us during the recruitment season to apply.

  • What role would I be assigned when I newly joined, and what would be my task?

    A new recruit would be assigned a role of “Research Fellow”, they would be trained on a specific topic and would start working on their assigned projects.

  • Can I add the work I did here to my resume?

    Yes, you can mention your work and contributions on your resume, and it will definitely serve as great value.

  • Are there any skills or qualifications required to apply for the R&D domain?

    There are no specific prerequisite skills or qualifications needed, a candidate with a basic understanding of any programming language (Python Preferred) ML, Computer Vision, and Mathematics till 12th grade are preferred.
